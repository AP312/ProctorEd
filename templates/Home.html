Want To Conduct Proctored Tests

If yes,then you are just one step away from conducting it.Welcome,we are team SmartCode presenting you ProctorEd.A safe and secure way of conducting online tests. ProctorEd provides varied ranges of quizzes with AI based proctoring facility.






Multi-combination Of Quizzes

ProctorEd provides you creation of varied ranges of quizzes with combinations of different types of question like MCQ,s , answer files , user inputs and coding tests.






Intelligent Proctoring Mechanism

ProctorEd provides AI based proctoring feature which can full screen capture , video capture , detect count of people in a room, detect face expressions and correspondingly issue warnings to the student. Videos of all the students in a meet will be displayed to quiz creator along with the warning issued.

Points To Note!!!

Create & Publish

Quiz master is able to create quizzes and generate meeting code. Candidates can join quiz by entering the meeting code followed by quick verification steps.

Be Alert You Are Proctored

Video of each candidate will be monitored by AI and displayed on quiz masters page.Warning will be issued if esc is pressed, two or more people in front of screen and if continuous mouth moment is detected.

Submission & Termination

User specific warnings will be visible to quiz master and termination facility is provided to them. Submissions of all candidates will be available on quiz master page.

Clarify Yourself

Candidate will also be able to communicate to quiz master for clearing doubts or for clarifying warnings via audio mute/unmute button.
